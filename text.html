About Me

Hello! I'm Anshul Diwakar, a dynamic and dedicated educator with a rich tapestry of professional experiences spanning multiple countries and diverse sectors. With five years of expertise at the National Securities Depository Limited (NSDL) in India, I honed my skills in finance and compliance, laying a strong foundation in organizational operations. My journey took me to the World Peace Organisation, where I spent four transformative years in South Korea and Thailand, working towards global harmony and understanding through various peace initiatives. Building on that experience, I contributed to the Universal Peace Federation for two years across with Indian and the USA Organisation, promoting interfaith dialogue and collaborative efforts for peacebuilding. Currently, I bring my wealth of knowledge and global perspective to the classroom as a teacher in Russia, where I inspire a new generation of learners through innovative teaching methods and a commitment to holistic education. My diverse background in education, finance, and peace advocacy uniquely positions me to foster a learning environment that encourages critical thinking, empathy, and cultural awareness among my students. Now I am working as a passionate web developer with skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I love creating beautiful and functional websites.
